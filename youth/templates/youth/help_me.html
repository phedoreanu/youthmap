FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Exploring Youth Map

  • What is YouthMap?

    YouthMap is an online tool for building projects by connecting ideas with resources. By using YouthMap you can:
    1. Have access to social ideas and resources
    2. Build your social projects easier
    3. Make positive social impact

  • YouthMap’s philosophy

    We believe that a better world is possible. It all starts from ourselves and the way we interact and communicate with the other around us. YouthMap offers the possibility for people to interact and share their ideas and resources for a better world. Concerning values, we know that the current financial crisis is a moral crisis. Behaviors based on consumerism, profit, exploitation, egoism are more common today than in the past. In conjunction with them, a radical ideological cultural and sexual revolution takes place, with beginning of 1960’s. Concepts such as equality of men and women as opposed to complementary, sexual rights as opposed to moral obligations as well as freedom opposed to responsible behavior, are the elements of the current social transformations.

    We believe in a new just order of the world, based on moral and humane values that can bring stability. Therefore YouthMap aims at bringing a positive change in the society by promoting the values in which it believes.

  • Affiliation

    YouthMap is a direct product of Youth Time Movement: www.youth-time.org, which created and supports its activity.
How it works?

  • Ideas

    If somebody has an idea but needs resources to transform it into a project, he can post it in this section.

    Examples

    - Organizing a day for cleaning rubbish from nature
    - Organizing a training course on web design
    - Recycling paper and transform it low price items for selling (cups, bags etc.)

  • Projects

    If somebody has a project but needs new ideas or resources to finish it, he can post it in this section

    Examples

    - The team for cleaning the rubbish from nature is ready, we just need local partners (NGO, recycling company) from the town of Sinaia, Carpathian mountains.
    - Training course on web design got the funds, we just need 2 experts on we design and programming
    - Recycling paper project: the agreement with the factory has been signed, we need volunteers for fundraising

  • Resources

    Catalog of organizations, Youthmap members, events, digital resources: here is the place to present and make them public available so the community can benefit from them.

    Examples

    - We need search for environmental NGO from Czech Republic
    - Event: Launching of a mobile application for volunteers
    - Online good practices database for fundraising

  • Is the service free?

    Yes, all is free. The only expectation from us is to see your projects transforming the society for better. And of course, to spread the word about the platform among your friends by using “Invite Promote” from Dashboard section.

  • What does YouthMap support?

    Our main fields of activity covers the following social related domains: Culture, Education, Environment, Health, New Media, Technology, Heritage, International projects in connection with volunteering and social entrepreneurship. We do not support projects connected with business, profit, politics or any ideological manifestations (moderate or radical).
Website navigation

  • The Filters

    They select the information on the map. All the ideas, projects and resources connected with the filter will be displayed on the map.

  • Google Map

    YouthMap’s visual concept is to use Google maps to geographically display the content. For this reason our website is called YouthMap.

  • Ideas section

    The place to present your idea or see other people’s ideas.

  • Project section

    The place to present your project or see other people’s projects

  • Resource section

    The place to present your resources or see other people’s resources. In this section you can browse Organizations catalog (all the organizations which registered on the website); YouthMap members (all the profiles registered), Events (all the actions which will take place in the near future) and New Media (everything related to online activity: blogs, webpages, music, photo, applications etc.)

  • Posting ideas/ projects/ resources

    You can do this by scrolling to the middle of Home page, Ideas, Project and Resources to the colored field called: “What to do next?”

  • Dashboard navigation

    Dashboard is the place where you control your profile and the related features.

  • My profile

    Contains “Basic profile” (the one which you completed at registration) and “Complete your profile” which is the full profile. We recommend for best results, to fully complete your profile.

  • My favorites

    Here you can see and control your favorites: all the ideas and projects which you set as favorite will be collected here.

  • Account settings

    From here you can control how your account works.
